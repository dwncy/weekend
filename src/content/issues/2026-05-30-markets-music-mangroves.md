Hey you two —

**Weekend of May 30 – June 1.** Warm and humid with a sea breeze near the coast. A weekend with a bit of everything.

## 1. Sunday market at Blok M
**Date:** Sun · morning
**Area:** South — Blok M
Produce, plants, and a surprising amount of vintage. Go for breakfast on the street first, then browse with no particular plan.
**Rough budget:** Rp 50–120k
**Weather:** humid, get there early
**Source:** [The Jakarta Post — What's On](https://www.thejakartapost.com)

## 2. Live music in Kemang
**Date:** Sat · night
**Area:** South — Kemang
Kemang still does the best low-key live night in the city — jazz, soul, a bit of everything. Pick a small room over a big club.
**Rough budget:** Rp 100–200k
**Weather:** indoor
**Source:** [Zomato — Kemang](https://www.zomato.com)

## 3. Mangroves and the coast
**Date:** Sun · afternoon
**Area:** North — PIK
Back to the boardwalk, then out to the PIK 2 promenade for sunset and seafood. The breeze off the water earns the drive.
**Rough budget:** Rp 30k + dinner
**Weather:** breezy by the coast
**Source:** [TripAdvisor](https://www.tripadvisor.com)
