Hey you two —

**Weekend of June 20–22.** It's a bright one: highs around 33°C, that thick Jakarta sun, with a decent chance of a short downpour after 3pm both days. Mornings are the move.

Here's what I found for us this week.

## 1. Pasar Santa, Saturday morning
**Date:** Sat · 9–11am
**Area:** South — Petogogan
Indie coffee roasters, vinyl crates and thrift racks stacked into one happily chaotic market. Go early, before the heat and the crowd settle in. Get a manual brew upstairs and people-watch.
**Rough budget:** Rp 75–150k for two
**Weather:** mostly indoor, fan-cooled
**Source:** [The Jakarta Post — What's On](https://www.thejakartapost.com)

## 2. Car Free Day on Sudirman
**Date:** Sun · 6–9am
**Area:** Central — Sudirman–Thamrin
The whole avenue closes to cars. Walk, jog, or just drift between the jajanan stalls with a kelapa muda in hand. It's the most alive the city feels all week, and it's free.
**Rough budget:** free–Rp 50k
**Weather:** go before 9, it turns hot fast
**Source:** [Jakarta Smart City](https://smartcity.jakarta.go.id)

## 3. A long Senopati brunch
**Date:** Sun · 11am onward
**Area:** South — Senopati
Reward the early start. Senopati is wall-to-wall cafés; grab a quiet corner, order too much, stay too long. Kaum if you want something properly Indonesian.
**Rough budget:** Rp 150–250k per person
**Weather:** indoor, AC
**Source:** [Zomato — Senopati](https://www.zomato.com)

## 4. Kota Tua at golden hour
**Date:** Sat · 4–6pm
**Area:** Old Town — Fatahillah Square
Rent one of the bright vintage bikes, loop the square, then duck into Museum Bank Indonesia before it closes. The light on the old Dutch facades around 5pm is the whole point.
**Rough budget:** Rp 50k
**Weather:** cooler after 4
**Source:** [Wonderful Indonesia](https://www.indonesia.travel)

## 5. PIK mangrove boardwalk
**Date:** Sun · 4–6pm
**Area:** North — Pantai Indah Kapuk
A genuinely green breather — herons, a quiet boardwalk over the water, the calmest hour of the weekend. Bring repellent. Pair it with dinner around PIK 2 after.
**Rough budget:** Rp 30k entry
**Weather:** breezy, some shade
**Source:** [TripAdvisor](https://www.tripadvisor.com)

## My top 3
1. **Pasar Santa** — for the coffee alone
2. **Car Free Day** — move your body before brunch
3. **Mangrove boardwalk** — the calmest hour of the weekend
