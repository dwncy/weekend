Hey you two —

**Weekend of June 6–8.** Mild and mostly dry — a rare gentle one. A good weekend to be outside and a little ambitious.

## 1. Kota Tua history loop
**Date:** Sat · morning
**Area:** Old Town — Fatahillah
Do the full circuit this time: the Jakarta History Museum, the wayang museum, then coffee at Café Batavia upstairs by the window. Go before 10 to beat the school groups.
**Rough budget:** Rp 50–100k
**Weather:** mild morning
**Source:** [Wonderful Indonesia](https://www.indonesia.travel)

## 2. Taman Suropati to the galleries
**Date:** Sat · late afternoon
**Area:** Central — Menteng
Start in the park with the buskers and turtles, then drift to a nearby gallery opening. Menteng on a clear evening is the quiet, grown-up version of a night out.
**Rough budget:** Rp 40k
**Weather:** lovely after 4
**Source:** [Jakarta Smart City](https://smartcity.jakarta.go.id)

## 3. Ragunan, slow Sunday
**Date:** Sun · 8–11am
**Area:** South — Ragunan
The zoo is enormous and green and best early. Bring water and decent shoes; the schedule of the primate center is worth timing around.
**Rough budget:** Rp 4k entry (yes, really)
**Weather:** shaded, warm by noon
**Source:** [Ragunan Zoo](https://ragunanzoo.jakarta.go.id)

## My top 3
1. **Kota Tua loop** — go early, go in
2. **Ragunan** — absurdly cheap, surprisingly restful
3. **Taman Suropati** — a soft landing into evening

---

*A note on sources:* hours for museums and the zoo shift on public holidays — worth a quick check.
