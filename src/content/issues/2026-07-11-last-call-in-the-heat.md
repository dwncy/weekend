**Weekend of July 11–12.** Jakarta still looks hot and humid, so you two should keep the plan cute, shaded, and easy to bail from if the clouds get dramatic. Make Jakarta Fair or MACAN the real anchor, then keep Ancol or Ragunan as the “only if we wake up with energy” option. Double-check official pages before leaving because ticket promos, hours, and lineups can shift fast.

## 1. Jakarta Fair final-weekend concerts
**Date:** 11–12 July 2026; check daily lineup
**Area:** North — JIExpo Kemayoran
**Why go:** This is the most “actually this weekend” pick: the fair is in its last stretch, with Juicy Luicy on Saturday night and NDX AXA on Sunday night, plus Barongsai slots and the usual snack-shopping chaos. Go hungry, walk slowly, and let the fair be the plan instead of trying to optimize every booth.
**Rough budget:** Paid entry + snacks/shopping
**Weather:** Best for late afternoon into evening; expect some outdoor walking between halls.
**Source:** [Jakarta Fair official site](https://www.jakartafair.co.id/)

## 2. Museum MACAN exhibition stack
**Date:** 13 June–4 October 2026; on this weekend
**Area:** West — AKR Tower, Kebon Jeruk
**Why go:** A clean indoor reset when the city feels sticky. One ticket currently covers Riar Rizaldi: Period Piece, Menelan Cakrawala, Marcos Kueh, Dawn Ng, and the children’s art space, so you two can wander, whisper hot takes, then leave before museum brain turns mushy.
**Rough budget:** Museum ticket; promos may apply
**Weather:** Safest heat-and-rain shelter; book/check ticket slots before going.
**Source:** [Museum MACAN tickets](https://www.museummacan.org/tickets)

## 3. Ancol seaside and mini-attraction loop
**Date:** Available 11–12 July 2026; check same-day promos
**Area:** North — Ancol / Pasar Seni / beach area
**Why go:** Keep it breezy and unserious: beach walk, Pasar Seni, maybe Bird Land or Sea World if you want something with air-con and structure. The trick is to choose one attraction, one snack, one slow sunset attempt — not the whole Ancol cinematic universe.
**Rough budget:** Rp 35k+ entry; attractions extra
**Weather:** Go morning or late afternoon; move indoors if rain clouds stack up.
**Source:** [Ancol official site](https://www.ancol.com/)

## 4. Ragunan green morning
**Date:** Available 11–12 July 2026; Tuesday–Sunday
**Area:** South — Taman Margasatwa Ragunan, Pasar Minggu
**Why go:** A soft, leafy morning before Jakarta gets loud. Walk a little, pick just a few animal zones, grab simple snacks, then leave while it still feels like a cute date and not a survival trek.
**Rough budget:** Low entry + transport/snacks
**Weather:** Morning only; bring water and do not force it if the sky looks dramatic.
**Source:** [Ragunan official site](https://ragunanzoo.jakarta.go.id/language/en/)

## If you only do three things
1. **Jakarta Fair final-weekend concerts** — the most date-specific, snack-heavy anchor.
2. **Museum MACAN exhibition stack** — the safest cool-down if it gets too hot or rainy.
3. **Ancol seaside and mini-attraction loop** — the best playful outdoor plan if the sky behaves.
