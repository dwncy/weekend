**Weekend of June 13–15.** Wetter than last week — expect on-and-off rain through Saturday and a clearer Sunday afternoon. I leaned indoor and cozy this time.

## 1. Sarinah, top to bottom
**Date:** Sat · afternoon
**Area:** Central — Thamrin
The revamped Sarinah is a whole rainy-day plan on its own: local makers on the lower floors, a food hall, and a rooftop that's lovely once the rain lifts. Easy to lose three hours here.
**Rough budget:** Rp 100–200k
**Weather:** fully indoor
**Source:** [Sarinah](https://sarinah.co.id)

## 2. M Bloc Space
**Date:** Sat · evening
**Area:** South — Blok M
Old state-printing warehouses turned into a courtyard of coffee, records, and little live sets. There's usually something on a Saturday night, and it's covered enough to dodge the drizzle.
**Rough budget:** Rp 80–150k
**Weather:** semi-covered courtyard
**Source:** [M Bloc Space](https://www.mblocspace.com)

## 3. Coffee crawl in Menteng
**Date:** Sun · late morning
**Area:** Central — Menteng
When the sky clears, Menteng's leafy streets are made for a slow walk between old houses and good cafés. Start at one of the classics and wander.
**Rough budget:** Rp 60–120k
**Weather:** clearer Sunday pm
**Source:** [The Jakarta Post — What's On](https://www.thejakartapost.com)

## My top 3
1. **M Bloc Space** — best odds of a good night
2. **Sarinah** — the rain-proof default
