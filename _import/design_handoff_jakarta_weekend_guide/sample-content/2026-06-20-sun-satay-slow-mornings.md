Hey you two —

**Weekend of June 20–22.** It's a bright one: highs around 33°C, that thick Jakarta
sun, with a decent chance of a short downpour after 3pm both days. Mornings are the move.

Here's what I found for us this week.

## 1. Pasar Santa, Saturday morning
**When:** Sat · 9–11am  ·  **Area:** South — Petogogan
Indie coffee roasters, vinyl crates and thrift racks stacked into one happily chaotic
market. Go early, before the heat and the crowd settle in.
**Rough budget:** Rp 75–150k for two  ·  **Weather:** mostly indoor, fan-cooled
**Source:** [The Jakarta Post — What's On](https://www.thejakartapost.com)

## 2. Car Free Day on Sudirman
**When:** Sun · 6–9am  ·  **Area:** Central — Sudirman–Thamrin
The whole avenue closes to cars. Walk, jog, or just drift between the jajanan stalls
with a kelapa muda in hand. The most alive the city feels all week, and it's free.
**Rough budget:** free–Rp 50k  ·  **Weather:** go before 9, it turns hot fast
**Source:** [Jakarta Smart City](https://smartcity.jakarta.go.id)

## My top 3
1. **Pasar Santa** — for the coffee alone
2. **Car Free Day** — move your body before brunch
3. **Mangrove boardwalk** — the calmest hour of the weekend
